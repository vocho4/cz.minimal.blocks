Privacy Policy – Minimal Blocks

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Introduction

Minimal Blocks is a simple offline game created for entertainment purposes. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used when you play the game Minimal Blocks.

Information Collection and Use

Minimal Blocks does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details

  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

The game works completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Third-Party Services

Minimal Blocks does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Minimal Blocks is suitable for all ages. Since the game does not collect any personal information, it fully complies with children’s privacy requirements.
